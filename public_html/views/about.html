Welcome to SourceMoz

SourceMoz (pronounced ‘sɔrs-mɒs’/‘sohrs-mahz’) is a simple and functional search engine that does not save, track or share your IP address or usage activity.

Most major search engines collect user information that is automatically sent to them by your computer including IP addresses, user agents, the time/date of the search and of course, what it is you searched for. Since a search engine is not required by law to collect or save any personal or usage information, at SourceMoz we choose not to do so.

Simplicity + Accessibility

At SourceMoz you’ll find a web search experience that is dedicated to both simplicity and accessibility, creating a dynamic and efficient UX that clearly identifies sponsored listings from organic search results, while minimizing distractions and unnecessary information.

SourceMoz Privacy Statement

This document was last updated on 4.15.2017.

SourceMoz is committed to protecting your privacy. By accessing sourcemoz.com, you acknowledge and fully understand this privacy statement and freely consent to the use practices described herein.

Online Privacy Protection

When using any website your web browser automatically sends specific information regarding your computer through the User Agent, as well as your IP address which can be used to identify who is performing the search. At SourceMoz we do not collect or store this information, which is central to providing an anonymous web search experience.

SourceMoz does not collect or store the following information under any circumstances:

- Your Internet Protocol (IP) address.

- Any information sent by the User Agent (browser type, operating system, computer software or hardware info).

- The city, state or country from where you accessed the site.

- The date and time of your visit.

- The name of your internet service provider (ISP).

- The web page you came to the site from.

- What pages are viewed on the site.

- What links are clicked on the site.

Cookies

SourceMoz provides the same interface to all visitors and does not use or install cookies to personalize or enhance the user experience.
